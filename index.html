COMMUNITY SWIMMING POOL

Dream Pop, Jangle Pop, Shoegaze, Alternative Rock, Indie Rock

Community Swimming Pool is the creation of multi-instrumentalist Glasgow-based dreampop musician Bmac. Heartbreaking autobiographical lyrics of depression and failed love paired with shimmering jangle guitars make for excellent dreampop escapism. Having written and recorded his debut single 'Upside Down' in one day, it then gained him an audience worldwide and support from numerous international radio stations including BBC Introducing. Back for more with his self produced second single Number One, the mood is somewhat lightened from the debut, shifting from real world current affairs to a whirlwind tale of love and the pain endured during efforts to find the right person.
